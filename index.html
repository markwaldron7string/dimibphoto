The Photographer

Rooted in attention.
Driven by light.

Photography, at its best, is an act of attention — the willingness to slow down, look closely, and find the extraordinary tucked inside the ordinary. That's what drives me.

I'm Dimitry, the photographer behind Dimi B Photography. I'm passionate about taking photos that tell your story — that showcase the unique spark that makes you you. Whether I'm framing a family at golden hour, capturing the gravity of a milestone moment, or chasing light across a canyon at dawn, my goal is always the same: images that feel true.

Based out of Northern Virginia — on the edge of a forest, which tells you something about how I see the world — my work spans portraits, events, travel, and landscape photography. A picture is worth a thousand words, and it's better when those words are full of love.
